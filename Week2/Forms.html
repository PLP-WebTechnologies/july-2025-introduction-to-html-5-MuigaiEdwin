Essential Farm Tasks

Daily and weekly tasks for successful livestock farming.

Daily Tasks

  1. Feed animals morning and evening
  2. Provide fresh clean water
  3. Check for sick or injured animals
  4. Collect eggs (for layers)
  5. Clean feeding areas

Weekly Tasks

  • Clean and disinfect housing
  • Check equipment and repairs
  • Plan feed purchases
  • Record production and expenses

Key Success Factors

Good Housing
Protect animals from rain, wind, and predators
Quality Feed
Use certified feeds appropriate for each animal type
Health Management
Vaccinate on schedule and treat sick animals quickly
Record Keeping
Track income, expenses, and production to improve profits
